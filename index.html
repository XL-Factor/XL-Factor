About XL-Factor

XL-Factor was created to solve the common pain points of working with Excel files. Our mission is to provide free, open-source tools that make Excel data processing easier for everyone.

All our tools run directly in your browser, ensuring your data remains private and secure. No uploads to external servers, no hidden costs.
